FAQ's

How Do I sing in to my Assis Connecting Account?

1. On your internet browser, go to the following link: https://sales.vestta.co/SALES/www_inicio.inicio  
2. Type in the username and password that was sent to your e-mail when the Assis Connecting service was initiated.  
3. Click on “Sign In”. 

Forgot your password?

1. Go to https://sales.vestta.co/SALES/www_inicio.inicio and click on “Forgot password?” link, located in the lower left side of the page. 
2. You will receive an email with a link to create a new password. Click on that link and follow the process. 

Note: For security purposes, this link will only be active for 2 hours. You can request this link as many times as needed. 
Recommendation: You must use an e-mail address that you read constantly and to which only you have access. It is important to keep your client’s information secure. 

I am not able to sign in with my username and password

This issue might happen due to different factors. The best way to identify the problem is by reading carefully the appearing message. 

1. If the message says: “Username or password incorrect”. Please validate the info you’ve got on the welcoming e-mail named “Welcome to Assis Connecting” and check if you are entering the correct data. 
2. If you’ve forgot your password. Please follow “Forgot your password” and create a new one. 
3. If the message says “The system is suspended”. Please contact member services 444-444-4444. One of our specialists will help. 

The application is too slow or it’s not loading 

The application is too slow or it’s not loading 

Try doing this: 
- Loading alternate web sites to verify if the browser stopped working
- Checking the router to validate if the internet is available. Please call your internet provider if needed. 
- If you are under a company network validate in your browser the proxy or firewall settings. 

The following links may help: 
- https://support.google.com 

The system is indicating the session has expired 

This is not an error. For your safety, the system will automatically sign you out after some time to prevent people from manipulating your information on your behalf. You just need to log back in. The timeout is approx. 60 minutes. 

How do I create a new customer?  

In the left menu of your screen: 
1. Click on “Sales” 
2. Click on “Customers” 
3. Fill out the form 
4. Click on “Create” button. 

How do I update an existing customer?

1. Filter your search. For ex. Filter by name to easier locate the customer. 
2. Once the customer is located click on “Consult” 
3. Select the customer from the results list 4. Update the fields required. 
5. Click on “Modify” button 

How do I create a new product? 

On the left menu of your screen: 

1. Click on “Inventory”. 
2. Click on “Items” 
3. Fill out the form 
4. Click on “Create” button. 

How do I update/modify an existing product? 

1. Filter your search. For ex. Filter by “Item’s Name” to easier locate your product. 
2. Once the product is located click on “Consult”. 
3. Select the product from the results list. 
4. Update the fields required. 
5. Click on “Modify” button. 

How do I report a new sale? 

On the left menu of your screen: 
1. Click on “Sales”. 
2. Click on “New Ticket” (SALE) (CASE) 
3. New customer. Follow “How do I create a new customer” 
4. Existing customer. Select the customer. 
5. Click on “Continue” button. 
6. Fill out the commercial form. 

WE ARE READY!!! The new ticket is created with a single serial number to be recognized and all the options available to move forward. 

How do I consult my sales? 

On your left menu of your screen: 
1. Click on “Reports” 
2. Click on “Sales Search”. 
You can filter the sales to find them quicker. A date range from: ____ to: ____, a specific customer and the current stage of the negotiation are the most common filtered searches. 
3. Click on “Consult” button. 

I have a payment, technical support or customer service concern 

1. Go to the site https://www.assisconnecting.com. Click on live chat where you can have immediate assistance. 
2. You can also click on “Support” button. Please fill out the fields required to create a new support ticket. One of our specialists will get in contact with you via phone or e-mail in a maximum of 30 minutes. From there, he/she will guide you until the problem is solved. 

Attention: You can also find answers to any of your questions in our quick guides and user manual “ASSISCONNECTING BIBLE”. https://www.assisconnecting.com/AssisConnectingbible
